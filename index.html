NotiLogs Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

NotiLogs is a utility app that stores a local history of notifications to help users recover notifications that were dismissed or lost after reboot.

Information Accessed

  • NotiLogs
  • Notification title
  • Notification content (if provided)
  • Timestamp

Data Usage

Notification data is processed and stored locally on the user's device. NotiLogs does not upload notification content to external servers.

Ads

NotiLogs may display banner advertisements to support development. Ads providers may collect limited device information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Control

Users can pause recording, delete notification history, and configure retention settings at any time.
